CRyptography and Information SECurity Laboratory (CRISEC)

University of Tsukuba, Department of Computer Science


Introduction

Our research group is studying theoretical aspects for cryptography and information security. Recent research topics include:
  • Cryptanalysis of Public Key Cryptosystems
    • Cryptanalysis based on number theory, algorithm and complexity theory
      • Cryptanalysis based on lattice reduction algorithms
      • Cryptanalysis under the secret key leakage
    • Cryptanalysis based on Quantum Computers
  • Proposal of Cryptographic Schemes
    • Proposal of Quantum-Safe Cryptographic Schemes
    • Proposal of High-functional cryptographic schemes and protocols
